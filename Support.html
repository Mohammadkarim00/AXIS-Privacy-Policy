About AXIS

AXIS Dental Laboratory is an iOS app for managing a dental lab's work — orders, patients, appointments, and the team — used by lab staff/administrators and doctors.

Common questions

  • I didn't get my verification code. Check your email's spam/junk folder, make sure the address is spelled correctly, then use "Resend code" on the verification screen. Codes expire after 10 minutes.
  • How do I create a doctor account? On the sign-in screen tap "Register as a doctor", enter your details, and confirm the code we email you.
  • I can't sign in / my account is inactive. Your lab administrator manages accounts. Ask them to confirm your account is active, or contact us.
  • How do I change my name, clinic, or contact details? Open Settings in the app and edit your profile.
  • How do I delete my account and data? A lab administrator can delete an account from the Users screen, or email us to request deletion.
